## Summary

A legacy two-page spec sheet for vertical and horizontal self-serve video ads and their companion images.

## Actionable markers

- Legacy maximum duration: 30 seconds; MOV or MP4; maximum 500 MB.
- Vertical reference: 9:16 at approximately 720x1280. Horizontal reference: 16:9 at approximately 1280x720.
- Legacy audio targets shown: -14 dBFS RMS and -0.2 dBFS peak; silent video was not accepted.
- Companion image reference: square, at least 640x640, JPG/PNG, maximum 200 KB.
